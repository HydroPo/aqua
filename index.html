Origin Story

How a Classroom Became a Startup

January 2025
💡

The Spark

In the middle of a chemistry lesson, two students asked a question that changed everything: "Why are we still farming the same way as 200 years ago?" The idea of putting a working hydroponic system inside a school wasn't just a class project — it was a bet on what food could look like in their city.

February 2025
🔩

Building the First System

With a small budget and zero experience in plumbing, Timur got to work with his hands. He measured, drilled, connected pipes, and wrestled with grow lights. Karim ran nutrient calculations and researched pH balances. Day one of testing — it leaked everywhere. They laughed, mopped up, and started over.

March 2025
💥

Failures That Taught Us Everything

A bad supplier sent broken LED panels. The first microgreen batch turned yellow from nutrient deficiency Karim diagnosed overnight. A pump failed twice at 11pm. Every failure taught us something the internet never could — resilience, the value of real chemistry knowledge, and why you always keep a spare pump.

April 2025
🚀

HydraPo Is Born

After months of rebuilding and growing, we harvested our first full crop. We opened the club to other students, turned the lab into a living classroom, and transformed a broken classroom experiment into a functioning startup. Today, HydraPo is not just a club — it is a movement.

🌱 First successful harvest — 2025
